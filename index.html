I'm a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in the Automated Reliable Intelligent Software Engineering Lab (ARISE Lab) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. My research interests lie in the reliability, robustness, and interpretability of deep learning models. To be more specific, I am interested in adversarial attack/defense algorithms in both CV and NLP areas. Now I am also a research intern at Tencent AI Lab in collaboration with Dr. Wenxiang Jiao and Dr. Zhaopeng Tu. Before joining the ARISE Lab, I was a research intern at SenseTime collaborating with Dr. Chen Qian and Prof. Bolei Zhou.

I obtained my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Peking University in 2019. I was a student from the Yuanpei College of Peking University. In Yuanpei College, students can choose any majors and take any courses in the entire University. This chance made me able to enjoy the knowledge not only in Computer Science, but also Economics, Social Psychology, and Philosophy.
