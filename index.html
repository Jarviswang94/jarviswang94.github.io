I'm a final year Ph.D. student in the Automated Reliable Intelligent Software Engineering Lab (ARISE Lab) in the Computer Science and Engineering Department (CSE) at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. My research interests lie in the reliability, robustness, and interpretability of deep learning models. Currently, I am interested in the gap between large language models and human beings regarding their psychology, decision-making, and other social behaviors. Now I am also a research intern in the NLP Center of Tencent AI Lab in collaboration with Dr. Wenxiang Jiao and Dr. Zhaopeng Tu.

I obtained my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Peking University in 2019. I was a student from the Yuanpei College of Peking University. In Yuanpei College, students can choose any majors and take any courses in the entire University. This chance made me able to enjoy the knowledge not only in Computer Science, but also Economics, Psychology, and Philosophy.
