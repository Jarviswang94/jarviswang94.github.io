I am an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at Renmin University of China. My research interest lies in the reliability and safety of Large Language Models.

Before joining Renmin University of China, I was a PostDoc Research Fellow in Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working with Prof. Shuai Wang. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2024, advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. I obtained my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

I was a research intern at the NLP Center of Tencent AI Lab, working closely with Dr. Wenxiang Jiao and Dr. Zhaopeng Tu.
